# 🌾 Agri Saathi

### AI Voice Assistant for Indian Farmers

**Voice-first • Multilingual • Smart Weather • Built for Bharat**

Agri Saathi is an AI-powered voice assistant designed to empower **140M+ smallholder farmers in India** by providing real-time, localized, and easy-to-understand agricultural insights. 

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Indian farmers face major challenges in accessing timely and useful information:

* 📱 **Low Digital Literacy**
  Complex apps and English-based interfaces are difficult to use.

* 📉 **Market Blind Spots**
  Farmers often sell crops at lower prices due to lack of real-time mandi data.

* 🌐 **Language Barrier**
  Most agricultural content is not available in regional languages.

* 🌱 **Crop Disease Losses**
  Late detection leads to **20–40% yield loss** every season. 

---

## 💡 Solution

**Agri Saathi — Speak. Listen. Grow.**

A voice-first AI system that enables farmers to interact in their native language.

### 🔑 Key Features

* 🎤 **Voice First Interaction**
  No typing required — farmers simply speak.

* 🧠 **AI Intent Engine**
  Automatically detects user intent:

  * Disease detection
  * Market prices
  * Weather queries
  * Reminders

* 🌦️ **Smart Weather Intelligence**
  Converts raw weather data into actionable farming advice.

* 🧾 **Context Memory**
  Remembers crop, location, and past queries for personalized responses. 

---

## 📲 User Journey

Simple and designed for **zero digital literacy**:

1. Open app
2. Enter mobile number → OTP login
3. Select language
4. Speak query
5. Get instant voice response (within seconds) 

---

## 🌦️ Smart Weather Intelligence

Transforms raw forecasts into farm decisions:

* 📍 Hyperlocal weather (village-level)
* ⚠️ Storm & rain alerts (3-hour advance)
* 💧 Irrigation advisory
* 🌿 Spray timing recommendations
* 🌾 Harvest timing alerts
* 📅 Seasonal crop planning
